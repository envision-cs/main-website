# Initial Concept

The Envision Construction Services main website is a modern, content-driven platform built with Nuxt. It serves as the digital front door for the company, showcasing their construction expertise in Florida, their "Built on Faith" core values, and their diverse portfolio of projects.

## Product Vision

To be the premier digital resource for potential clients and talent in the Florida construction market, effectively communicating Envision's unique value proposition of quality, reliability, and community impact.

## Target Audience

- **Potential Clients:** Decision-makers looking for construction management, design-build, and specialty project services in regions like Tampa Bay and Sarasota.
- **Potential Employees:** Skilled professionals and recruits seeking a values-driven company culture and opportunities for growth in the construction industry.

## Business Goals

- **Lead Generation:** Convert site visitors into project inquiries through clear service descriptions and accessible contact points.
- **Brand Authority:** Establish Envision as an industry leader by showcasing a high-quality project portfolio and sector-specific expertise.
- **Recruitment:** Build a strong employer brand that attracts top-tier talent by highlighting the team and company culture.

## Key Features

- **Project Portfolio:** A filterable gallery of completed and ongoing projects, categorized by sectors (Athletics, Business, Arts, etc.).
- **Service Showcases:** Dedicated pages for core offerings like Construction Management, Design-Build, and Preconstruction.
- **Meet the Team:** Dynamic profiles of team members and teams (Green, Blue, Orange, etc.) to showcase the people behind the projects.
- **Values Integration:** Prominent display of core values like "Built on Faith" and "Versatile" across the user journey.

## Brand & Tone

- **Professional & Reliable:** Instilling confidence through a clean, modern design and clear communication of expertise.
- **Community-Oriented:** Emphasizing local roots in Florida and a faith-based approach to business and construction.

Home to the largest Private Garage Community in the world, this new office headquarters is an interior unit build-out from shell. The 2,714 SF office features six office spaces, four bathrooms, three removable partition walls, two showers, two conference rooms, a washer and dryer, a technology room, phone booth, kitchen, common area, and a second-level balcony overlooking the track.
